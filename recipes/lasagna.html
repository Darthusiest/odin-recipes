Lasagna

Description

This is a faster, and less expensive lasagna. That can be completed in a hour. With 30 minutes of prep and 30 mins of cooking you can have a total of 8 servings prepared for lunch or dinner. A large skillet and oven will be needed. Oven must be preheated to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C).

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes
  3. Drain
  4. Pre heat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  5. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper;drain.
  6. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.
  7. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
